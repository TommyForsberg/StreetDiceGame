Rules

First roll.
To win in the first round you either has to roll "Seven Out"(sum of seven) or "Yo-leven"(sum of eleven). However if you roll "Snake Eyes", "Ace Deuce" or "Midnight" you loose instantly.

The following rolls.
In case you don't hit any of the outs in the first round the game will continue. The goal for the following rounds is to hit the number that you rolled in the first round. For example if you hit 9 you will have to hit 9 again. However if you roll "Seven Out"(sum of seven), then you loose. The game continues until you either hit your target or the dreaded "Seven Out".

Bet.
In case you win you will win your bet. In case you loose you loose your bet.

Pickpocket.
You can try to steal a 'stick' if you're low on money. You have a 3/5 chance to succeed in your theft. In that case you win one stick. If you fail, you're discovered so you will loose all of your sticks in bet and some of the sticks (0 -> total) in your wallet.
